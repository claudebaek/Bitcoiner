Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

BitcoinMax ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience:

  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • App feature usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crash reports

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

1.2 Local Data

The following preferences are stored locally on your device only:

  • Language preference
  • Auto-refresh settings
  • Haptic feedback preferences
  • Mining calculator settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Important: BitcoinMax does NOT collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or financial data. We do not have access to your Bitcoin wallet information.

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous analytics data we collect is used to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Plan future feature development

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) - For anonymous usage analytics
  • CoinGecko API - For Bitcoin price data (no personal data shared)
  • Mempool.space API - For blockchain data (no personal data shared)

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect any data collected:

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not store any personal data on our servers

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track" in iOS Settings
  • Request information about data collection by contacting us

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
